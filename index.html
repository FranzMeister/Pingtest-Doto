    What is a ping tester and how does it benefit me?

    People often complain about lag. Lag in reference to video games is just a delay between you and the server. Sometimes lag spikes (great delay that occurs [repetitively]) are often caused by the gameservers itself or caused by your ISP or your own connection. Most of the times the lag spikes are caused by your own connection because you were downloading stuff or someone in your household is using up all the bandwidth that is available.

    So by doing a ping test you will measure how long it takes (in milliseconds) for a data package to be sent from your connection to the receiving end. The less delay you have the better is your ping!
